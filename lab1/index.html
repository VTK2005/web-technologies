Welcome to VIT-AP — A hub for learning and research

VIT-AP (Vellore Institute of Technology, Amaravati) is committed to excellence in education, research and innovation. Discover programs, campus life and placement support.

About VIT-AP

VIT-AP is part of the VIT group, offering a modern curriculum, strong industry ties and a focus on research-led teaching. The campus supports interdisciplinary collaboration and student entrepreneurship.

Research & Placements

Faculty-student research collaborations, centers of excellence and funded projects. The placement cell connects students with national and global employers for internships and full-time roles.

Campus Life

Modern labs, student clubs, sports, accommodation, cultural events, hackathons and workshops — plenty of opportunities to learn and grow outside the classroom.
